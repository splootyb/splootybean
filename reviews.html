Review: Rec Room

  Rec Room is a free, online, virtual-reality cross-platform game for PlayStation, and PC via Steam. In Rec Room, you experience everything in a first-person view! Play quests such as ^GoldenTrophy, ^CrimsonCauldron, ^JibbledPlants, and ^TheRiseOfJumbotron. Play sports like ^Dodgeball, and ^Paintball. Join rooms created by other players, play with friends, and more! We would give Rec Room a 7.5 out of 10.

 

Review: Super Smash Brothers: Ultimate

  Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is an amazing fighting game with over 80 characters, beautiful graphics, and addictive gameplay. It's accessible to all skill levels and has a variety of modes, including online multiplayer. A must-play for fans of Nintendo and fighting games. We would give Super Smash Brothers: Ultimate a 9.8 out of 10.
